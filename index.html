Our Story

The Story of Nonna Lina

"NONNA LINA - Cucina Italiana" was born from love, memory, and tradition.

Orsolina Pampalone—affectionately known as Nonna Lina—was born in 1935 in Custonaci, Trapani, Sicily. She cooked every day from scratch; her recipes inspired the flavors we serve today.

In 2023, Nonna Lina passed away, leaving behind a legacy that could never be forgotten.

We bring traditional recipes into a modern, fast-casual experience—imported pasta, quality ingredients, and flavors that tell a story.

Because great food doesn’t need to be complicated — just real.

Our Promise

We prepare everything from scratch using quality ingredients, honoring tradition while delivering fast-casual convenience. Honest food, made with care.
